Relative Clauses — Choisir le bon pronom

📌 Rappel grammatical

who — personnes, sujet · whom — personnes, objet (formel) · which — choses, sujet/objet · that — personnes/choses, restrictif (jamais après une virgule) · whose — possession · where — lieu · when — temps · why — raison

Defining (pas de virgule, information essentielle) vs Non-defining (virgules, information complémentaire — that interdit).

RC002
  1. 1.We talked about the party. Sarah wants to organize it for my birthday. We talked about the party that/which Sarah wants to organize for my birthday.
  2. 2.Mrs Flowers has paintings in her house. They are worth over $10,000. Mrs Flowers has paintings in her house that are worth over $10,000. Mrs Flowers has paintings which/that are worth over 10,000 in her house.
  3. 3.The couple lives next to us. Their daughter has two charming boys. The couple, whose daughter has two charming boys, lives next to us.
  4. 4.This is the house. I had my first party here. This is the house where I had my first party.
  5. 5.Jerry received a bad mark on his essay. It was only one page long. Jerry received a bad mark on his essay, which was only one page long.
  6. 6.Ingrid went to see the doctor yesterday. He is a friend of hers. Ingrid went to see the doctor, who is a friend of hers, yesterday.
  7. 7.Mr Reynolds had to do all the work himself. His secretary left a few days ago. Mr Reynolds, whose secretary left a few days ago, had to do all the work himself.
  8. 8.The boy took the photograph. He gave it to his mother. The boy took the photograph, which he gave to his mother.
  9. 9.That is the man. I met him at Alice’s party. That is the man who/whom I met at Alice’s party.
  10. 10.The boy smiled. Elena gave him some money. The boy who/whom Elena gave some money (to) smiled.
  11. 11.Steven is an architect. His designs have won many awards. Steven, whose designs have won many awards, is an architect.
  12. 12.Decorating is a difficult job. I hate it. Decorating is a difficult job which/that I hate. I hate decorating, which is a difficult job.
  13. 13.Christmas is a day. People are happy. Christmas is a day when people are happy.
  14. 14.We went to the restaurant. We had met there before. We went to the restaurant where we had met before.
  15. 15.The woman called yesterday. She wants to buy the house. → The woman who wants to buy the house called yesterday.
RC003-RELATIVE-CLAUSES
  1. 1.The police arrested the man. He murdered his wife. The police arrested the man who murdered his wife.
  2. 2.I can't remember the name of the street. I lost my passport there. I can't remember the name of the street where I lost my passport.
  3. 3.Where are the shoes? I bought them yesterday. Where are the shoes (that, which) I bought yesterday.
  4. 4.The book is very interesting. I borrowed it from the library. The book (that, which) I borrowed from the library is very interesting.
  5. 5.I'll never forget the day. I got my first bike on that day. I'll never forget the day (when) I got my first bike.
  6. 6.This is the man. His dog bit me. This is the man whose dog bit me.
  7. 7.The pupils missed the test. They came late. The pupils who came late missed the test.
  8. 8.This is my boyfriend. His father works at Macy's. This is my boyfriend whose father works at Macy's.
  9. 9.That man is a politician. I forgot his name. That man, whose name I forgot, is a politician.
  10. 10.I'll show you the church. I got married there. I'll show you the church where I got married.
  11. 11.The actor is very famous. I met him a few days ago. The actor who (whom) I met a few days ago is very famous.
  12. 12.I have a new camera. I bought it last week. I have a new camera which I bought last week.
  13. 13.I passed the entrance test. That is quite difficult to do. I passed the entrance test, which is quite difficult to do.
  14. 14.My friend invited many people. His house is huge. My friend, whose house is huge, invited many people.
  15. 15.John belongs to a group of people. They send food packages to Africa. John belongs to a group of people who send food packages to Africa.

RC005-RELATIVE-CLAUSES
  1. 1.That is the man. I spoke to him the other day. That is the man (who, whom) I spoke to the other day.
  2. 2.They elected a new mayor. His aim is to help the poor. They elected a new mayor whose aim is to help the poor.
  3. 3.I really like the car. I bought it last year. I really like the car (that, which) I bought last year.
  4. 4.Mr Black took over the company. His wife died last year. Mr Black, whose wife died last year, took over the company.
  5. 5.The restaurant is the best in town. We usually eat there. The restaurant where we usually eat is the best in town.
  6. 6.The film is about a woman. Her son mysteriously disappears. This film is about a woman whose son mysteriously disappears.
  7. 7.That is the place. I grew up there. That is the place where I grew up.
  8. 8.I met a woman. Her husband is a famous producer. I met a woman whose husband is a famous producer.
  9. 9.The man was arrested yesterday. He escaped from prison a week ago. The man who escaped from prison a week ago was arrested yesterday.
  10. 10.The bag is heavy. It contains a lot of presents. The bag, which contains a lot of presents, is heavy.
  11. 11.The Marybelle Hotel is opening up again. I stayed there a few years ago. The Marybelle Hotel, where I stayed a few years ago, is opening up again.
  12. 12.My friend's father gave us the tickets. He owns a travel agency. My friend's father, who owns a travel agency, gave us the tickets.
  13. 13.Mr Honeywell is a good teacher. Everyone likes him. Mr Honeywell is good teacher, whom everyone likes. Mr Honeywell, who everyone likes, is a good teacher.
  14. 14.The exercise was very complicated. Nobody could do it. The exercise which nobody could do was very complicated.
  15. 15.That's the producer. His films are always a great success. That's the producer whose films are always a great success.
